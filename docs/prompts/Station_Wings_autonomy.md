## Lessons Learned

### Prompt Writing Best Practices Demonstrated

1. **Use Memorable Metaphors**
   - "Give Station Calyx its wings" - vivid, aspirational
   - Creates emotional connection
   - Defines transformation clearly

2. **Define Clear Transformation**
   - "from ground-based to autonomous flight"
   - Before and after state
   - Direction of change

3. **Require Concrete Evidence**
   - Measurable autonomy percentages
   - Success rate tracking
   - Demonstratable capabilities

4. **Cover Multiple Dimensions**
   - Decision-making
   - Self-healing
   - Learning
   - Efficiency

   - Confidence scoring
   - Escalation pathways
   - Human oversight
