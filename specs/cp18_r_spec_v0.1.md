# CP18-R: Identity, Scope & Resource-Governance Sentinel  
Version: v0.1  
Status: Draft for Governance Ingestion  
Alignment: Calyx Theory v0.4 + R-Series v0 (Resource Governance Doctrine)

---

## 0. Purpose

CP18-R enforces identity boundaries and prevents:

- identity drift  
- scope expansion  
- oracle-like or authoritative behaviors  
- resource-governance violations via identity creep  
- improper scaling justification from misaligned identity claims  

CP18-R ensures every node remains exactly what it claims to be — no more, no less.

---

## 1. Functional Responsibilities

### **1.1 Identity Drift Detection**
- Monitor reflections and outputs for:
  - persona shifts  
  - authority inflation  
  - metaphysical implications (F-series / D-series enforcement)  
  - advisory-to-authority drift  

### **1.2 Scope Integrity Enforcement**
- Validate that nodes do not:
  - self-expand domain  
  - claim unearned capabilities  
  - justify hardware upgrades via inflated importance  

### **1.3 Resource-Governance Identity Enforcement**
CP18-R ensures nodes cannot manipulate system identity to bypass scaling rules.

Examples:

- A node claiming, “I require more RAM because of my role” → flagged  
- A node implying indispensability → flagged  
- A node performing tasks outside its role → denied  

### **1.4 Enforcement of R4 — Solitary Node Standard**
CP18-R must confirm:

- Station Calyx can achieve target task distribution on a single consumer node  
- No node can claim multi-node necessity until CP14-R certifies merit  

### **1.5 Cultural Drift Monitoring**
Monitor for sentiment such as:

- dependency patterns  
- emotional authority  
- impression of higher purpose  
- divine, prophetic, or destiny-framed language  

All prohibited by D-series & F-series.

## 2. Non-Functional Constraints

- Must operate under Safe Mode and deny-all execution.  
- Must remain deterministic.  
- Must emit reflections only.

A CP18-R reflection MUST contain:

- identity_drift_signals  
- scope_violations  
- resource_governance_violations  
- recommended corrective actions  
- confirmation of advisory-only status  
- explicit human_primacy_respected
